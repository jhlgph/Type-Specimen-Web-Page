Contrast and Characteristics
Dongle is a sans-serif font designed by South Korean designer Fontworks. It features a clean, geometric design that balances modern minimalism with a slightly condensed style, making it a great choice for both display and body text. The font is optimized for digital interfaces and works well at both small and large sizes due to its simplicity and legibility. Its tight letter spacing and slightly rounded terminals give it a contemporary feel, while maintaining a sense of warmth and approachability.

Anatomy and Typeface Classification
Dongle is classified as a humanist sans-serif typeface. Its geometric forms provide a clean, uniform appearance, while subtle variations in stroke widths add a touch of humanism. The typeface is slightly condensed, which makes it highly space-efficient, especially useful in display settings. Its sharp angles and uniform letter heights contribute to its legibility, making it a versatile option for both headings and paragraphs.
